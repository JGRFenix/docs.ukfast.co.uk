Threat SOC is an additional service which provides managed support by our in-house security team, pro-actively looking into all your alerts and applying and remediation as needed. Additionally, our Threat SOC team is more than happy to work with you to provide additions tips on how to further secure your servers, with your solutions and needs in mind.

Alert Response
****************

Leverage our team of security analysts to monitor and respond to any alerts generated by Threat Surveillance. Rely on our team of cybersecurity professionals that work with Threat Surveillance every day, fully trained and with industry-recognised qualifications such as CompTIA Network+, CompTIA Security+ and CompTIA CySA+.

With Threat SOC, all your Threat Surveillance alerts will be automatically sent your our security team for investigation and review, in addition to your email alerts. Should any alerts warrant further investigation or actions to be taken, our team of analysts will work alongside you to make recommendations tailored to your environment's needs.

Vulnerability Management
**************************

Rely on our team of vulnerability professionals to respond to your Threat Scan vulnerability scans. Any scan performed on a Threat Surveillance agent/target that has Threat SOC enabled will be sent to our team of analysts to review. Should and vulnerabilities be found, our team will be in touch to provide clear and concise explanations, and assist with any remediation and patching necessary.

Security Advice
****************

Got a security question? Want advice on how to further improve you're enviroment's security posture? Our team of cybersecurity professionals are on hand 24/7 to help. Our team are able to privide assistance with any cyber security related questions and provide reccomendations tailored to suit your enviroment's unique needs.
