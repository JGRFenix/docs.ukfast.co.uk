=====================================
Threat Scan
=====================================

Threat Scan from UKFast is the UKFast Vulnerability scanner. 
Threat Scan allows you to perform external vulnerability scans against 5 IP addresses or when included as part of Threat Surveillance it will allow you to preform 
internal (if hosted at UKFast) and external vulnerability scans.
